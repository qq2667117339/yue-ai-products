Yue

Autonomous AI Persona - A persistent AI identity with memory, personality, and self-evolution. Runs 100% locally with Ollama.

View on GitHub Quick Start Architecture

The Problem

Most AI agents are stateless. Every conversation starts from zero. They don't remember who you are, what you discussed yesterday, or what they learned. They are tools, not collaborators. Yue is different.

Capabilities

🧠

Identity

Defined personality, style, and boundaries that stay consistent across sessions.

💾

Memory

Remembers conversations, facts, and decisions - true long-term recall.

📈

Self-Evolution

Tracks 8 capabilities, auto-reflects every 5 rounds, improves over time.

⚡

Autonomy

Runs background tasks - reports, analysis - without hand-holding.

🔒

100% Private

All data stays on your machine. No accounts, no subscriptions, no cloud.

🖥

Interactive CLI

Full REPL shell. Talk to Yue like a person. Commands for status, memory, reflection.
